Before I start listing my favorite products, here are a few things I look for in a makeup product that I want to use everyday

  • Afforable: I buy majoritiy of my make up on sale. If I buy a higher end product, I like to make sure it'll last me a long time. I believe that there are so many hidden gems in the drugstore.
  • Inclusive Shade range: As a dark skin black girl, finding products that flatter my skin tone is super important. Everyone deserves to have a product that works for them
  • Long Wearing: I don't like having to retouch my face throughout the day and as a student, especially in the middle of a pandemic where facemasks are worn, longevity is key.

Here Are A Few of My Go To Products!

Clinique Beyond Perfecting Foundation + Concealer

I absolutely LOVE this foundation! It's $31, which is pretty afforable for a department store foundation. Here are a few highlights

  • It has a medium coverage that can be made to full coverage
  • The finish is very natural, leaning towards a satin finish
  • It has 40 shades so it's super inclusive
  • I love the applicator, make it super easy to spot treat and your hands won't get that messy with foundation

Fenty Pro Filt'r Instant Retouch Setting Powder

I'm a huge fan of Fenty Beauty by Rihanna, but this powder in the shade nutmeg is one of my favorites. Heres why:

  • Lovley matte finish, but it's not drying like some powders
  • While it is not drugstore priced ($32) A little goes a LONG way. I have had this powder for almost two years and i haven't even used a fifth of the product!
  • There is an excellent shade selection. Rihanna NEVER misses when it comes to inclusion
  • passes the flash test! no need to worry about looking like this :/

Lets talk Highlighters!: Maybelline & Wet N Wild

I absolutely love the Maybelline Master Chrome Highlighter in "molten gold" and Wet N Wild Mega Glo Highlighter in "awesome blossom" There was a time when it was SO hard to find nice drugstore highlighters, I'm so glad the drugstre has elevated their game, because I was not ready to spend $40 on a highlighter here are a few pros of these two:

  • The Maybelline highlighter is super bright and intense, I love using it as a nose highlight.
  • The Wet N wild highlighter can be very natural and also very intense if you want it to be. It's very buldable.
  • Both of these brands has an amazing color selection that caters to fair skin and dark skin

Fenty Gloss Bomb

Although it is expensive ($18) the Fenty Beauty Gloss bomb is such a great product to treat yourself with! Rihanna has done a great job and creating a color selection where everyone can have their own perfect nude. My favorite is Hot Chocolit, it's the perfect nude for me.

  • It gives you the "wet" look and stays glossy for a long time.
  • The way it shimmers on the lips is perfect. Not to gritty, just the perfect amount of shine and glow.
  • Moisturizing formula.

Now Let's talk Eyeshadow!

The Anastasia Beverly Hills X Jackie Aina eyeshadow palette is a perfect eyeshadow palette if you are looking for a pigmented eyeshadow palette that you can do gorgeous everyday looks, as well as night time looks. If you want something more afforable, Juvia's Place has some amazing eyeshadow palettes that are vibrant, pigmented and afforable plus they're a black owned brand.

I absolutely love makeup, and These were just a few of my essentials that I use to make a nice base and a solid makeup look. Makeup is so much fun and you don't have to break the bank to do a great look!
